Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our Business Command Center collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our application. By using this app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when using the app, including:

  • Profile information (name, email, birthdate)
  • Employment information (start date, team, role)
  • Time tracking and attendance data
  • Task assignments and communications
  • Time off requests

3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This means:

  • Data persists only on devices where you are logged in
  • Clearing browser data will remove your information
  • Data is not transmitted to external servers

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

6. Third-Party Services

This application is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices. We do not integrate with other third-party services that collect personal data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact your HR administrator or system administrator.

Disclaimer: This Privacy Policy is a template and should be reviewed by legal counsel before use in production. It may not comply with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
